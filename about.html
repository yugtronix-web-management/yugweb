About YugTroniX

Who We Are

YugTroniX is the official Robotics Club of the Department of Mechanical Engineering at JNTUK UCEK. We are a team of passionate innovators, engineers, and thinkers working at the intersection of mechanical design, electronics, and programming.

Our club provides a platform for students to explore, experiment, and build robotics projects — from concept to creation — while developing both technical expertise and leadership skills.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help students learn robotics through real, hands-on projects. We believe in collaboration, interdisciplinary learning, and solving real-world problems through creativity and teamwork.

At YugTroniX, it's not just about technical knowledge — we also help students become independent and confident innovators. We encourage you to bring your ideas to life and take action, rather than waiting for direction. We support initiative, self-driven work, and thinking beyond the ordinary.

Our Vision

We aim to become a well-known, student-led robotics club that creates impactful and smart engineering solutions. Our goal is to make a difference in both academics and the real world.

We believe that every student has the potential to lead. At YugTroniX, we create an environment where you are free to explore new ideas, take initiative, and grow your skills. This is a place where creativity, fresh thinking, and innovation are always encouraged.

What We Do

At YugTroniX, our activities revolve around innovation and practical learning in the field of robotics and technology:

  • Design and build autonomous robots and drones
  • Work on embedded systems using Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and ROS
  • Organize workshops, hackathons, and technical fests
  • Promote cross-disciplinary innovation and practical learning
  • Participate in national and regional robotics competitions
  • Provide mentorship and guidance to new members

Our hands-on approach ensures that members gain practical experience while working on cutting-edge technology projects.

Flagship Projects

QuadDrone

An advanced quadcopter drone, controlled by joystick and hand gestures with vibration reduction for improved stability. Features a custom flight controller and LIDAR integration for enhanced mapping. The drone operates indoors without GPS reliance.

Future Scope: Adding airbags and a gripper, vision-based navigation system.

Autonomous FPV Car

A self-driving first-person view (FPV) RC car that navigates autonomously while providing real-time video feedback. This platform integrates computer vision, machine learning, and robotics to explore autonomous vehicle concepts.

6 DOF Robotic Arm

A functional robotic arm with 6 degrees of freedom capable of performing tasks such as picking and placing objects with precision and control. This project gives members hands-on experience with kinematics, control systems, and mechanical design.
